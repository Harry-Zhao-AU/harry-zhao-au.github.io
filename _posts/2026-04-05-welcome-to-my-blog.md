Welcome to my blog! I'm Harry Zhao, a software developer, and this is my little corner of the internet.

## Why I Started This Blog

I've always believed that writing about what you learn is one of the best ways to solidify your understanding. This blog is my way of documenting my journey as a developer — the things I build, the problems I solve, and the lessons I learn along the way.

## What to Expect

I'll be writing about:

- **Software Development** — tutorials, code walkthroughs, and best practices
- **Tools & Technologies** — reviews and guides for the tools I use
- **Project Deep Dives** — behind-the-scenes looks at my projects
- **Career & Growth** — reflections on growing as a developer

## Built with Jekyll

This site is built with [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) and hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/). It's simple, fast, and lets me write posts in Markdown — exactly what I wanted.

```ruby
# Even the config is simple
title: Harry Zhao
description: "Software Developer | Blog & Portfolio"
```

Stay tuned for more posts. Thanks for stopping by!
